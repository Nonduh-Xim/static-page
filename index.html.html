Key tips for modeling

Posing

Learn your best angles and expresion by in front of a mirror

Use props and walls to create a more dynamic poses by angling your limbs

Walking

Practice walking, weather in heel or flats. maintain your posture while at it and walk into the music

At times the heel may come off, make sure you find a way to blance in an "imaginary" heel as the show must go on
